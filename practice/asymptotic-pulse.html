Asymptotic Pulse

Coming Soon

A heart-rate-responsive film installation built on an Adafruit Feather ESP32-S3 and BioAmp Pills, reading a viewer's live BPM to control a video's playback speed and volume in real time through VLC. The film — assembled from family archival footage, genealogy documents, and original performance — traces a family history of forced displacement and its aftermath. By giving viewers physiological control over how a traumatic memory unfolds, the project asks what it means to offer an audience command over an experience that was never theirs to control — and, like its namesake asymptote, an approach that is felt but never fully arrives.
