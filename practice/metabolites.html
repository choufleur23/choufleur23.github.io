Metabolites

Coming Soon

In the 2010s, an entire clan's villages and homes were demolished to make way for an industrial park, and the community was relocated into urban residential complexes. Today, the land where they once lived processes electronic waste — waste embedded with rare earth metals and shattered screens, generated in part by the very people displaced to build the park. Their locations and relationships, once circulating as data inside the devices they discarded, now return to the ground that raised them.
