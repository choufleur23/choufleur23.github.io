2021 · Documentary Short · Director, Producer, Cinematographer & Editor

The Dream Café

Set in a Shanghai café that hires only young workers with mental disabilities, this film documents the life of Yin Hao, a coffee maker with Down syndrome whose talent and optimism defy expectation.

Winner, 2021 Quill and Scroll Documentary Category (International & China Domestic) · Winner, 2021 New Jersey Documentary Film Festival, High School Student Film · Official Selection, Hellbender Student Film Festival (2021), All American High School Film Festival (2020), Arlington International Film Festival (2020)
