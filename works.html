2025 · VR Documentary Short · Director & Editor

Whispers of the Grid

Grown out of my undergraduate honors thesis at Cornell, this documentary follows residents of Texas "Bitcoin towns" living alongside industrial crypto-mining operations. It pairs traditional documentary technique with an immersive VR companion piece, drawing on sensory ethnography, eco-cinema, and Foucault's writing on infrastructure and power to ask how energy infrastructure reshapes landscape, economy, and identity.

2024 · Installation & Performance · Creator

The Web

An installation and performance built from discarded materials and the image of the spider's web, staged with performer Natalina Putrino. The piece treats trash and thread as parallel infrastructures — one abandoned, one built to catch — and asks what each reveals about entanglement and repair.

2024 · Documentary Short · Director & Editor

Synchrotron Symphony

A poetic portrait of Cornell's underground particle accelerator, cutting between the machinery, cabling, and structure of the lab below ground and the physical training of the football team above it at Schoellkopf Field. The film builds a sensory, audiovisual argument for what scientific infrastructure can mean when seen — and heard — differently.

Winner, 2024 Cornell University Milstein Program "Art + Tech" Competition · Finalist, 2024 Raw Science Film Festival · Official Selection, 2024 China International Conference of Science and Education Producers

2023 · Documentary Short · Co-Director & Director of Photography

Morning Dew: The Experience

Produced alongside the exhibition "Morning Dew: The Stigma of Being Brainwashed" by artist Soni Kum at Cornell's Herbert F. Johnson Museum of Art, this documentary conveys the experience of the multimedia exhibit and its focus on the lives of Zainichi Koreans — ethnic Koreans resident in Japan.

2023 · Experimental Short · Director, Cinematographer & Editor

Chronirvana

A twenty-year-old college student confronts her chronophobia — a fear of time — and is led to question the purpose of her own existence. The film renders linear time nonlinearly, contemplating recent change and the instability of identity.

2021 · Fiction Short · Director

The Murderess

Hannah falls in love with Casey, until a classmate's warning and a background check surface Casey's troubled past. A confession reveals a secret implicating both Casey and her accuser — and asks whether blind trust can survive an internet-dominated era.

2021 · Documentary Short · Director, Producer, Cinematographer & Editor

The Dream Café

Set in a Shanghai café that hires only young workers with mental disabilities, this film documents the life of Yin Hao, a coffee maker with Down syndrome whose talent and optimism defy expectation.
