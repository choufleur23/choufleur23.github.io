About

Shuqian Lyu (b. 2003, Suzhou, China) is a filmmaker and media artist based in Durham, NC and the San Francisco Bay Area. Her work merges technology, performance, and ethnography to explore the connections between humans and nonhumans, bringing scientific inquiry into dialogue with philosophy and lived experience. She studied at the United World College of the Atlantic in Wales before earning a B.A. with distinction and highest honors from Cornell University, and is now an MFA candidate in Experimental and Documentary Arts at Duke University. Her work has been supported by the Duke Graduate School, the Mellon Foundation, the Cornell Council for the Arts, Cornell in Sundance, and the Telluride Film Festival Student Symposium. She is also a dedicated triathlete and rock climber.
