About

Shuqian Lyu (b. 2003, Suzhou, China) is a filmmaker based in Durham, NC and the San Francisco Bay Area. Her work traces how people and places are shaped by environmental change, institutional control, and historical rupture, merging documentary, technology, and critical inquiry.

She studied at the United World College of the Atlantic in Wales before earning a B.A. summa cum laude from Cornell University, and is now an MFA candidate in Experimental and Documentary Arts at Duke University.

Her work has been supported by the Duke Graduate School, the Mellon Foundation, the Cornell Einhorn Center, and the Cornell Council for the Arts. She is a 2026 Flaherty Seminar Fellow. She is also a dedicated triathlete and rock climber.
