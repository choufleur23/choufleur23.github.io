Whispers of the Grid

Grown out of my undergraduate honors thesis at Cornell, this documentary follows residents of Texas "Bitcoin towns" living alongside industrial crypto-mining operations. It pairs traditional documentary technique with an immersive companion piece, drawing on sensory ethnography, eco-cinema, and Foucault's writing on infrastructure and power to ask how energy infrastructure reshapes landscape, economy, and identity.
